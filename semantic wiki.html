League of Legends

League of Legends League of Legends (or LoL; initially League of Legends League of Legends: Clash of Fates) is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) genre video game developed by Riot Games. It was designed to operate on the Microsoft Windows operating system. The game was first announced on October 7, 2008 and released a year later on October 27, 2009.The game was in beta from April 10, 2009 to October 26th, 2009.

Summoner Spells

    Heal

    - Heal is an auto-targeted summoner spell that instantly restores health to your champion and an ally while also granting a small burst of movement speed.

    Ghost

    - Ghost is a self-targeted summoner spell that increases your champion's movement speed and grants them the ability to ignore unit collision for a few seconds.

    Barrier

    - Barrier is a self-targeted summoner spell that shields your champion from damage for a few seconds.

    Exhaust

    - Exhaust is a unit-targeted summoner spell that slows an enemy champion's movement speed and reduces their damage dealt for a few seconds.

    Flash

    - Flash is a ground-targeted summoner spell that causes your champion to blink a short distance in the direction of the cursor.

    Teleport

    - Teleport is a unit-targeted summoner spell summoner spell that blinks your Champion champion to the target allied unit after a few seconds of Channeling channeling.

    Smite

    - Smite is a unit-targeted summoner spell, it deals true damage to the target large or medium monster, enemy minion, or pet and heals you when cast on large monsters.

    Cleanse

    - Cleanse is a self-targeted summoner spell that removes most crowd control effects and summoner spell debuffs.

    Ignite

    - Ignite is a unit-targeted summoner spell that deals Hybrid true damage over the duration and applies Grievous Wounds healing reduction as well as granting Sight sight of the targeted enemy Champion champion.

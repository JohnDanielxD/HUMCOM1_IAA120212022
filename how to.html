How to cook Filipino Style Spaghetti

Ingredients

  1. 2 lbs. Spaghetti noodles
  2. 1 lb. ground beef
  3. 4 pieces hotdogs or beef franks
  4. 35 ounces Filipino Style Spaghetti Sauce
  5. 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  6. 1 1/2 cups beef broth
  7. 1 medium onion minced
  8. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  9. Salt and pepper to taste
  10. 300ml Evaporated milk
  11. 250ml All purpose cream
  12. 3 tablespoons cooking oil

    13. Instructions

    1. Boil water and when it boils put a little salt then put the pasta in the casserole.
    2. When the pasta is cooked, remove the casserole from the stove, drain it, transfer the pasta to another pan, then set it aside.
    3. Now, in preparing the sauce. Using a pan, saute the garlic first. When the garlic turns golden brown, add the onions.
    4. Once the onions are soft and cooked, add the ground beef. Then, put a little bit of salt and pepper.
    5. When the beef is completely cooked add hotdog and del monte spaghetti sauce.
    6. When its almost done, put evaporated milk and all purpose cream to make it more creamier.
    7. Pour the sauce into the pan containing the pasta. Top it with grated cheese.
    8. Serve. Share and enjoy!
